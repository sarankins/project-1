Sea Salt Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of salted butter
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips
  • Flaky sea salt

Step 1: Gather Ingredients

Before you start baking, preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) Make sure you have all your ingredients prepped and ready.

Step 2: Prepare the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugars until smooth.
  2. Add in the egg and vanilla and mix thoroughly.
  3. Once combined, scrape down the mixing bowl and add in flour, baking soda and salt. Mix again until fully combined.
  4. Add in chocolate chips and do a rough mix.

Step 3: Shape the Cookies

Shape the dough into small balls, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet.

Step 4: Bake the Cookies

Bake the cookies for 13-15 minutes or until golden brown.

Step 5: Enjoy!

When they are done baking, sprinkly flaky sea salt on top so it sticks as they cool. Let cool for 5-10 min, pour a cup of cold milk, and dunk those cookies! YUM!!
